The girl who makes everything warmer.

Tepat 20 tahun lalu, seorang wanita cantik nan lucu lahir ke dunia ini. Dunia tidak langsung tahu betapa beruntungnya ia kedatangan seseorang seperti ini. Tapi aku tahu.

She carries a thousand questions wherever she goes.The curious kind that notices everything, the small print, the odd detail, the thing everyone else walked past. Restless in the best way: always wondering, always looking a little deeper.
Hyperactive everywhere, but somehow calm with me.She's the loudest person in any room, and then somehow, with me, she just settles. I don't know if that's comfort or coincidence. But it felt like being chosen, quietly.
A complete package, without the fine print.Being loved by her doesn't come with conditions. No judgment, no performance required. Just two people who somehow make sense together.
